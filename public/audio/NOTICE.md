# Projector advance sound

`carousel-advance.mp3` is adapted from **Slide projector #3**, sound 2965,
recorded by Joseph Sardin and published on BigSoundBank:

https://bigsoundbank.com/slide-projector-3-s2965.html

The source recording is dedicated to the public domain under
[CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
Attribution is not required, but is included here with thanks to the creator.

The bundled version has been trimmed, accelerated to match the visual shutter,
gently filtered, faded, and encoded as a mono MP3.
